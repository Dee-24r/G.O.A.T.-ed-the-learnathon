What is this?

One weekend. A whole new skill set.

G.O.A.T.'ed is a 2-day learnathon where you pick up a handful of new technologies and turn them into real projects. You'll learn as much as you can over the weekend, then finish it all off with your own capstone project to show off.

FAQ

Who can join?

Any teen aged 13–18. If that's you, you're in!

Do I need coding experience?

Nope! We're super beginner-friendly and will teach you to code from scratch.

How much does it cost?

Nothing — it's totally free for all participants.

Is it online or in-person?

In-person, in New York City. (Exact venue coming soon.)

Do I need a team?

Nope — don't worry about having a team before the event. Teens form teams together once we're there.

Will meals be served?

Yes! Lunch and dinner will be served on both days.

What should I bring?

Just yourself and your curiosity. We'll share a full list closer to the event.

Still have a question or want to reach out?

Send us a message and we'll get back to you.

Become a mentor

We're looking for mentors to spend the weekend with small groups of teens, helping them get unstuck on their projects and cheering them on — no lecturing required. If you enjoy building things and explaining them to curious people, you'll fit right in. Come for a few hours or the whole weekend, whatever works for you.
