What is this?

One weekend. A whole new you. 🚀

G.O.A.T.'ed is a 2-day learnathon where you pick up a pile of brand-new technologies and turn them into real projects. The more you learn, the closer you get to GOAT status — then you finish it all off with your own capstone project to show off.

Questions?

Who can join?

Any teen aged 13–18. If that's you, you're in! 🎉

Do I need coding experience?

Nope! We're super beginner-friendly and will teach you to code from scratch.

How much does it cost?

Nothing — it's totally free for all participants.

Is it online or in-person?

In-person, in New York City. (Exact venue coming soon.)

Do I need a team?

Don't worry about it — teens team up with teens and we'll help sort it out on the day.

Just yourself and your curiosity. We'll share a full list closer to the event.
